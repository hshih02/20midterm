Looking at visual art through the lens of sexual misconduct

A panel discussion sponsored by the Boston Art Dealers Association (Co-sponsored by the Trustman Art Gallery at Simmons College)

The #MeToo movement has touched upon many sectors of our society, including the world of visual art. How do our opinions and perceptions of artists accused of sexual misconduct change? What are the challenges in acknowledging accomplished work in this light, and what are the outcomes for both the artist and the public? Please join us for a discussion with:

  • Samuel Adams
    Koch Curatorial Fellow, deCordova Sculpture Park and Museum

  • Sarah Kennel
    Byrne Family Curator of Photography, Peabody Essex Museum

  • Steve Locke
    Artist and educator, Massachusetts College of Art

  • Karen Moss
    Artist and collector

  • Alicia Rodriguez-Alvisa
    Photographer, video and performance artist
